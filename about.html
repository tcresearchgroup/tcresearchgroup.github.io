About Our Research Group

Discover our story, mission, and values

Our Story

The Tri-Chandra Research Group (TCRG) was established in 2018 with a vision to promote scientific research and innovation at Tri-Chandra College, one of Nepal's oldest and most prestigious educational institutions. What began as a small initiative by a few passionate faculty members has since grown into a vibrant research community.

In our early days, we focused primarily on building research capacity and infrastructure within the college. Through persistent efforts and collaborations with national and international partners, we have expanded our scope and impact over the years.

Today, TCRG serves as a hub for scientific exploration and academic excellence, bringing together students, faculty, and researchers across various disciplines to pursue innovative research and contribute to Nepal's scientific development.

Our Mission

To advance scientific knowledge through innovative research, foster a collaborative academic environment, and develop the next generation of researchers and scientific thinkers in Nepal.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in scientific research, providing impactful solutions to local and global challenges while establishing Tri-Chandra College as a leading research institution in South Asia.

Our Values

  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships across disciplines and institutions
  • Inclusivity: Providing opportunities for all talented individuals regardless of background

Our Achievements

2018

TCRG Foundation

Established the Tri-Chandra Research Group with initial focus on physics and mathematics research.

2019

First Research Publication

Published our first research paper in the International Journal of Physics, focusing on quantum computing applications.

2020

NAST Research Grant

Secured a significant research grant from the Nepal Academy of Science and Technology to study renewable energy applications.

2022

International Collaboration

Established research partnerships with universities in India, China, and Japan, expanding our global research network.

2023

Research Lab Expansion

Inaugurated our new advanced physics laboratory with state-of-the-art equipment for experimental research.

2025

Innovation Award

Received the National Innovation Award for our work on sustainable energy solutions for remote Himalayan communities.
