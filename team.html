Our Team

Meet the brilliant minds behind Tri-Chandra Research Group

The People Who Make It Happen

Our team consists of dedicated faculty members, research associates, students, and alumni who work collaboratively to advance scientific knowledge and innovation.

Executive Committee

Dr. Rajendra Adhikari

Chairperson

Quantum Physics

Dr. Adhikari leads the Tri-Chandra Research Group with expertise in quantum physics and computational methods. He has published numerous papers in international journals and is committed to advancing research culture in Nepal.

Dr. Balkrishna Jha

Vice Chairperson

Material Science

Dr. Jha is a leading researcher in material science with a focus on nanomaterials and their applications. He has been instrumental in establishing research collaborations with international institutions.

Dr. Narayan P. Chapagain

Secretary

Space Physics

Dr. Chapagain specializes in space physics and atmospheric science. His research focuses on understanding ionospheric phenomena and their effects on communication systems.

Dr. Sanjeev Marhatha

Treasurer

Biophysics

Dr. Marhatha's research lies at the intersection of biology and physics, with a focus on biomolecular interactions and their applications in medicine.
