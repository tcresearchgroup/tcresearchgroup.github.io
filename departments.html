Department of Physics

Exploring the fundamental laws of the universe through rigorous research and education

About the Department

The Department of Physics at Tri-Chandra Research Group was established in 2017 to advance the frontiers of physical sciences through innovative research and educational initiatives. Our department focuses on both theoretical and experimental physics, with specialized research groups working in areas including quantum physics, plasma physics, astrophysics, and condensed matter physics.

Our team consists of 12 faculty members, 8 research associates, and numerous graduate and undergraduate students engaged in cutting-edge research projects. We collaborate with national and international institutions to address important scientific questions and technological challenges.

Mission

To advance scientific understanding of physical phenomena through rigorous research, provide excellent education to the next generation of physicists, and apply physical principles to address real-world challenges in Nepal and beyond.
